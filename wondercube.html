About WonderCube

WonderCube is my submission for Ludum Dare / LDJam 37. It is a game in which you must bring your green cube to the purple goal by rotating the larger cube they both sit in. WonderCube gains amusement from your antics.

Ludum Dare is a 72 Hour Game Jam where contestants must make the best game they can, given a certain theme, within the time limit. WonderCube is my submission. I used few external assets. Almost everything was made by me, within 72 hours.

I may add further levels to WonderCube, but for now is stands as an interesting experiment to see how much I could accomplish in a short amount of time.
