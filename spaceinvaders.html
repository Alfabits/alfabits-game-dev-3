About Space Invaders: REDACTED

Space Invaders: REDACTED is a re-imagining of the classic 2D arcade game "Space Invaders".
REDACTED focuses less of strategic placement in slow combat and focuses more on being able to react
quickly to quickly changing hordes of aline invaders as you mash the shoot button for your life.

I made this game in four days as part of a college assignment. The goal was to make a game that
best captured lots of game feel, and REDACTED does that very well. Shooting the aliens feels very good,
and players get to have a lot of that visceral feedback in short intervals because the only limit to
how fast they can shoot is their own ability to move their finger.

I'm planning on expanding this game with new content and features sometime soon.
