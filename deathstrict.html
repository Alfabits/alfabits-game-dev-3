About Deathstrict

Deathstrict is a third person vehicle shooter by Virtual Void Studios. I acted as one of the primary programmers,
and developed important pieces of the game, including the Game UI, Player HUD, and Split Screen Sound System.

Virtual Void Studios was formed to create Deathstrict, which was the Capstone game we made for Algonquin College's
Game Development program. We made it as our Capstone Project, and it was well received at showings such as LevelUp! and Algonquin's End-of-Year showfloor.

Deathstrict is currently finished development, and there are no plans to produce it further.
