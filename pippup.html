About Pip-Pup

Pip-Pup is a mobile, virtual pet game where you take care of a robotic puppy and teach it how to love. I spent a couple of months making it as a gift for a family member, though it is still, technically in progress.

All of the assets in Pip-Pup were developed by myself, as a part of Alfabits.

I will likely add more features to the game as time goes on, as the framework is all completed. All that's left is to add content.
